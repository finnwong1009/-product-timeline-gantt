# Product Launch Planning

Use this skill for this repository's product planning work: 产品规划, 产品策划, 上市节点策划, version planning, launch readiness, and cross-functional milestone plans.

## Core Workflow

1. Clarify the planning object: product, version, feature, market, audience, expected launch window, and decision owner.
2. Build the planning frame: target user, scenario, problem, value proposition, business objective, success metrics, assumptions, and constraints.
3. Define scope: must-have, should-have, out-of-scope, dependencies, compliance or channel requirements, and known risks.
4. Map launch nodes backward from上市/GA: strategy lock, PRD lock, design lock, engineering freeze, QA entry, beta, supply/content readiness, channel readiness, launch approval, launch day, post-launch review.
5. Produce an execution artifact suited to the request: planning memo, launch milestone table, checklist, risk register, or decision log.
6. End with explicit open questions, owner gaps, and next actions.

## Output Standards

Prefer structured Chinese outputs unless the user asks otherwise.

For product planning, include:
- 背景与目标
- 用户/场景/痛点
- 产品定位与核心价值
- 范围与优先级
- 上市路径与关键节点
- 成功指标
- 风险与依赖
- 待决策事项

For上市节点策划, use a table with:
- 阶段
- 节点
- 时间/相对周期
- 交付物
- 负责人/协同方
- 准入标准
- 风险

For reviews, prioritize missing decisions, unclear owners, invalid sequencing, unrealistic timing, unmeasured goals, and launch readiness gaps.
